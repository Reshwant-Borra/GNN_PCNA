# MD Validation Strategy

## Strategy

Do not use MD as validation by default. MD requires pre-registered questions, setup provenance, comparable systems, and claim limits.

## Required before MD interpretation

Pre-MD reality check, hypothesis, setup manifest, force field/protonation/input provenance, analysis plan, allowed positive and negative claim language.
