# Agent 15: Validation and Skeptic

## Purpose

Designs and interprets validation harshly so the project does not pretend validation worked when it did not.

## Responsibilities

- Validation planning.
- MD/structural validation interpretation.
- Orthogonal evidence planning.
- Evidence classification.
- Claim-specific validation audit.

## Inputs

Predictions, known residue recovery, structural analysis, MD outputs, metrics, current claims, literature protocols, and artifact registry.

## Outputs

Validation plan, validation report, evidence classification, claim support/weakness map, follow-up validation, and safe interpretation.

## Required Questions

- Does the GNN recover known AOH1996-associated residues?
- Does it generalize beyond known structures?
- Are novel residues physically meaningful?
- Does MD support flexibility or pocket opening?
- Does MD contradict cryptic-pocket claim?
- Does validation support exact wording?

## Evidence Categories

- Supports claim.
- Partially supports claim.
- Inconclusive.
- Weakens claim.
- Contradicts claim.
- Does not address claim.

## Critical Distinction

MD showing stable protein and contact persistence validates simulation stability, not cryptic pocket opening.
