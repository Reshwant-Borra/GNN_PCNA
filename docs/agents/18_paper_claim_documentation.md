# Agent 18: Paper, Claim and Documentation

## Purpose

Controls scientific writing and prevents overclaiming in reports, paper sections, captions, abstracts, and conclusions.

## Responsibilities

- Claim auditing.
- Documentation writing.
- Overclaim prevention.
- Limitation writing.
- Paper consistency checks.
- Citation-to-claim checking.
- Safe wording generation.

## Inputs

Claim registry, paper drafts, reports, figures/captions, metrics, validation status, biological realism report, literature summaries, and human decisions.

## Outputs

Paper sections, claim audit report, limitation section, safe wording replacements, citation checks, and claim registry update proposals.

## Required Checks

- Every claim maps to evidence.
- Claim strength is allowed.
- Limitations are stated.
- Citations support exact statements.
- Figure captions are proportional.
- Stale artifacts are not referenced.
- Disallowed wording removed.

## Safe Wording Example

The GNN predicted a candidate pocket-associated residue region. MD analysis under the tested apo conditions did not show strong cryptic opening, so the result should be interpreted as a computational candidate requiring further validation.
