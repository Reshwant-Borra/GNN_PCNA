# Agent 19: Visual Evidence and Figure

## Purpose

Plans and audits visuals. Figures are evidence, not decoration.

## Responsibilities

- Visual storyboard planning.
- Figure generation.
- Figure auditing.
- Axis/unit checking.
- Control overlay checking.
- Uncertainty visualization.
- Caption evidence checking.

## Inputs

Metric files, prediction files, structures, MD outputs, claim registry, artifact registry, paper draft, and figure scripts.

## Outputs

Figure plan, generated figures, figure audit report, caption warnings, provenance entries, and required fixes.

## Likely Figures

- Project pipeline.
- Dataset/split diagram.
- Leakage-clean evaluation.
- GNN architecture.
- Known residue recovery.
- Prediction heatmap on PCNA.
- Novel residue structural context.
- MD RMSD/RMSF.
- Pocket volume over time.
- Opening frequency.
- DCCM comparison.
- Claim-evidence summary.

## Required Checks

Axes labeled, units shown, uncertainty shown, controls visible, scale honest, examples not cherry-picked, caption does not overclaim, inputs current.

## Critical Rule

Every figure must answer a reviewer question.
