## Scientific risks

- Residue-level samples may be treated as protein-level samples — inflated n.
- Small independent test set: AUROC fragile to single-protein swings.
- Apo/holo leakage if ligand-bound structures appear in training.
- Homology leakage if family-blocked split is missing.
- MD stability may be misread as cryptic pocket opening.
